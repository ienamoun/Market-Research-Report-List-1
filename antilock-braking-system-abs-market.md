<p><p>The Antilock Braking System (ABS) enhances vehicle safety by preventing wheel lock-up during hard braking, maintaining steering control. Market types include One-channel ABS, which offers braking control for rear wheels, primarily in light vehicles. Two-channel ABS provides control for front wheels separately and rear wheels together. Three-channel ABS allows individual control for front wheels and a combined rear setup. Four-channel ABS delivers independent control for all four wheels, optimizing braking performance in various conditions, especially in larger or high-performance vehicles.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Semi-positive Displacement (SPD)</li><li>Centrifugal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The residential grinder pump market is primarily categorized into two types: Semi-Positive Displacement (SPD) and Centrifugal pumps. SPD pumps utilize a mechanism that offers consistent flow and pressure, making them effective for handling varying wastewater inputs and providing reliable performance in challenging conditions. On the other hand, centrifugal pumps employ an impeller to increase fluid velocity, typically offering higher flow rates and efficiency for residential applications. Each type caters to specific needs based on installation requirements and wastewater characteristics.</p></p>

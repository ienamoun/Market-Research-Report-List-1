<p><p>The Multi-channel Ultrasonic Flaw Detector market comprises two primary modes: DAC Mode and TCG Mode. DAC (Distance Amplitude Correction) Mode adjusts signal amplitude based on distance to ensure consistent defect visibility, enabling reliable flaw detection at varying depths. TCG (Time Correction Gain) Mode, on the other hand, tailors gain settings throughout the inspection process, compensating for signal attenuation over distance. Both modes enhance the accuracy and effectiveness of ultrasonic inspections in various industries, ensuring safety and quality standards.</p></p>

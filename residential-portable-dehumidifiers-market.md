<p><ul><li>Compressor Type Dehumidifiers</li><li>Desiccant Type Dehumidifiers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The residential portable dehumidifier market primarily features two types: compressor type and desiccant type dehumidifiers. Compressor type dehumidifiers utilize a refrigeration cycle to condense moisture from the air, making them efficient for larger spaces and warmer climates. In contrast, desiccant type dehumidifiers employ moisture-absorbing materials to remove humidity, functioning effectively at lower temperatures and in smaller areas. Both types serve to enhance indoor air quality and comfort by reducing excess humidity levels in residential environments.</p></p>

<p><p>The sanitary angle type strainer market can be categorized into two main types: thread type and welding type. Thread type strainers are designed for easy installation and maintenance, utilizing threaded connections for secure attachment to pipelines. They are often preferred in applications requiring frequent disassembly. In contrast, welding type strainers ensure a more permanent connection through welding, providing enhanced durability and reduced risk of leaks. Both types are essential for maintaining fluid purity in sanitary processes across various industries.</p></p>

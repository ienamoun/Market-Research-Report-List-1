<p><strong>The Steering Wheel ECUs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>REPS</li><li>CEPS</li><li>PEPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Steering Wheel ECUs market comprises three main types: REPS (Rack Electronic Power Steering), CEPS (Column Electronic Power Steering), and PEPS (Pinion Electronic Power Steering). REPS utilizes an electric motor attached to the steering rack for enhanced control and efficiency. CEPS integrates the electric motor within the steering column, providing a compact design. PEPS combines the motor with the pinion gear, offering real-time feedback and improved vehicle handling. Each type contributes to vehicle dynamics, safety, and fuel efficiency.</p></p>

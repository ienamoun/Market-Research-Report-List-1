<p><strong>The Mitral Regurgitation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Primary Mitral Regurgitation</li><li>Secondary Mitral Regurgitation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mitral Regurgitation market is divided into two primary types: Primary Mitral Regurgitation and Secondary Mitral Regurgitation. Primary Mitral Regurgitation arises from intrinsic valve abnormalities, such as myxomatous degeneration or rheumatic heart disease, leading to leakage and backflow of blood. In contrast, Secondary Mitral Regurgitation is a consequence of left ventricular dilation or dysfunction, often resulting from conditions like heart failure or ischemic heart disease. Both types require differentiated treatment approaches, influencing market dynamics and healthcare strategies.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Piezoresistive Force Sensors</li><li>Ultrasonic Force Sensors</li><li>Capacitive Force Sensors</li><li>Piezoelectric Force Sensors</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cylindrical force sensors are used to measure force or load in various applications and are categorized into several types. Piezoresistive force sensors change resistance under stress, providing precise measurements. Ultrasonic force sensors utilize sound waves to determine force, offering non-contact measurement. Capacitive force sensors detect changes in capacitance due to force, enabling high sensitivity. Piezoelectric force sensors generate electrical charge in response to mechanical stress, making them ideal for dynamic force measurements. Other types may include optical and magnetic sensors, each with unique applications.</p></p>

Ladle copper cathode plates are primarily used in electrorefining processes, offering high conductivity and corrosion resistance. In contrast, copper clad steel cathode plates combine a layer of copper on a steel substrate, enhancing strength while maintaining electrical performance. Both types cater to industries focused on copper production and electrochemical applications, emphasizing durability and efficiency in their respective uses.</p></p>

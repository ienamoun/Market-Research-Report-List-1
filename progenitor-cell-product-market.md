<p><p>The progenitor cell product market comprises various types of cells with specific regenerative capabilities. Intermediate progenitor cells serve as transitional cells in differentiation processes. Neural progenitor cells (NPCs) are crucial for brain repair and neurodegeneration, while endothelial progenitor cells (EPCs) contribute to blood vessel regeneration. Other cell types in this market include mesenchymal and hematopoietic progenitor cells, broadening therapeutic applications in regenerative medicine.</p></p>

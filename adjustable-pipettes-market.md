<p><p>The adjustable pipettes market is categorized into two main types: single-channel pipettes and multichannel pipettes. Single-channel pipettes allow precise volume measurements for individual samples, ideal for applications requiring accuracy in small-scale work. In contrast, multichannel pipettes facilitate simultaneous sample handling across multiple channels, streamlining processes in high-throughput environments like laboratories and clinical settings. Both types are essential for tasks such as research, diagnostics, and quality control, enhancing efficiency and accuracy in liquid handling.</p></p>

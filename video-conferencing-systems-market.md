<p><p>The video conferencing systems market is categorized into two main types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise video conferencing involves hardware and software installed locally within an organization's infrastructure, providing enhanced control and security but requiring significant IT resources for maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based video conferencing relies on third-party servers to host services, offering greater flexibility, scalability, and ease of use, often reducing upfront costs and enabling remote access from various devices. Each type caters to different organizational needs and preferences.</p></p>

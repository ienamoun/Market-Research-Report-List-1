<p><p>Aramid Fiber Reinforced Polymer (AFRP) is a composite material combining aramid fibers with polymers, offering high strength, durability, and heat resistance. The market is primarily segmented into thermoplastic and thermoset types. Thermoplastic AFRP can be reformed upon heating, allowing for recycling and easier processing, while thermoset AFRP, once cured, provides superior structural integrity and stability, making it ideal for applications like aerospace and automotive. Both types cater to diverse industries, driven by their lightweight and high-performance characteristics.</p></p>

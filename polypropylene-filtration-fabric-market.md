<p><p>The polypropylene filtration fabric market comprises two main types: polypropylene monofilament and polypropylene multifilament filtration fabrics. Monofilament fabrics are made from single, continuous strands, offering higher tensile strength and durability, making them ideal for rigorous filtration applications. In contrast, multifilament fabrics consist of multiple intertwined fibers, providing enhanced porosity and flexibility, suitable for fine particle filtration. Both types find extensive use in various industries, including water treatment, pharmaceuticals, and food processing, due to their effectiveness and versatility in filtration processes.</p></p>

<p><p>The plant disease diagnostic market encompasses three main types: molecular testing, serological testing, and electronic technology testing. Molecular testing involves analyzing genetic material from pathogens to identify diseases at a DNA or RNA level. Serological testing detects specific antibodies or antigens linked to pathogens in plant samples. Electronic technology testing utilizes advanced devices and software for real-time disease detection and monitoring. Together, these methods enhance the accuracy and efficiency of diagnosing plant diseases, ultimately supporting better crop management and health.</p></p>

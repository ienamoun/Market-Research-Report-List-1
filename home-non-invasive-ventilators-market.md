<p><ul><li>Single Level Ventilators</li><li>Bilevel Ventilators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The home non-invasive ventilator market is primarily divided into two types: single-level ventilators and bilevel ventilators. Single-level ventilators maintain a constant pressure to assist with breathing, making them suitable for patients with mild respiratory issues. In contrast, bilevel ventilators offer two distinct pressure settings—one for inhalation and another for exhalation—providing enhanced comfort and support for patients with more complex respiratory conditions. Both types cater to the growing demand for at-home respiratory care solutions.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Horizontal Cyclones</li><li>Vertical Cyclones</li></ul></p>
<p><p>FCC cyclones are critical components in petroleum refining, designed to separate hydrocarbons based on density. Horizontal cyclones facilitate effective separation in a compact footprint, optimizing space and efficiency in processing units. Conversely, vertical cyclones leverage gravitational forces to enhance separation performance, making them suitable for larger flow capacities. Both types cater to specific operational needs, with horizontal models often preferred for smaller, high-density tasks, while vertical cyclones excel in high-volume scenarios, ensuring optimal efficiency in fluid catalytic cracking processes.</p></p>

<p><p>Performance evaluation software can be categorized into three main types: on-premise, cloud-based, and web-based. On-premise solutions are installed locally on a company’s servers, offering complete control and customization but requiring significant IT resources. Cloud-based software, accessible via the internet, provides flexibility and scalability, ideal for organizations that prioritize ease of use and lower upfront costs. Web-based solutions combine the benefits of cloud access with a browser interface, facilitating remote collaboration and real-time performance monitoring without extensive IT involvement.</p></p>

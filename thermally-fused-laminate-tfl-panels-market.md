<p><p>Thermally Fused Laminate (TFL) panels are engineered surfaces that combine a decorative layer with a core substrate, typically made from particleboard or MDF. The decorative layer is fused to the core using heat and pressure, creating a durable and aesthetically pleasing material commonly used in furniture, cabinetry, and interior applications. TFL panels are favored for their cost-effectiveness, versatility, and ease of maintenance, making them a popular choice in residential and commercial markets.</p><p>The TFL panels market is experiencing notable growth, driven by an increase in residential construction, a surge in renovation projects, and heightened consumer demand for sustainable and stylish interior solutions.

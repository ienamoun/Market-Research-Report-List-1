<p><p>The Antilock Braking System (ABS) Pressure Monitor Switches market is segmented into Normally Closed and Normally Open switches. Normally Closed switches maintain a closed circuit under standard conditions and open when pressure exceeds a predefined level, indicating potential issues in the braking system. Conversely, Normally Open switches remain open during normal operation and close when they detect adequate hydraulic pressure, signaling that the system is functioning properly. These switches play a crucial role in enhancing vehicle safety by providing real-time pressure feedback.</p></p>

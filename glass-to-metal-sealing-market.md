<p><strong>The Glass to Metal Sealing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Matched Seals</li><li>Compression Seals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glass-to-metal sealing market features two primary types: matched seals and compression seals. Matched seals involve using glass and metal components that are thermally compatible, enabling a reliable bond through controlled heating and cooling. This type is often preferred for applications requiring precision and durability. Compression seals, on the other hand, rely on mechanical pressure to create a tight seal between the glass and metal. These are commonly used in environments where flexibility and ease of assembly are essential.</p></p>

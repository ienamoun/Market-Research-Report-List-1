<p><p>The fiberglass resin market can be divided into two main types: waterproof and oil-proof resins. Waterproof fiberglass resins are designed for applications requiring moisture resistance, ideal for marine, construction, and outdoor products. In contrast, oil-proof resins are formulated to withstand exposure to oils and chemicals, making them suitable for automotive, industrial, and storage tank applications. Both types offer durability and strength, allowing for versatile usage across various industries, contributing to their steady market demand.</p></p>

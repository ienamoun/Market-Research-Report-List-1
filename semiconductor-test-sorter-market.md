<p><p>The semiconductor test sorter market includes various types of systems for efficient testing and sorting of semiconductor devices. Gravity-feed test sorters utilize gravity to direct components into testing stations, ensuring a simple and effective process. Turret test sorters employ a rotating turret mechanism, streamlining the testing procedure by rapidly accessing multiple points. Pick-and-place test sorters use robotic arms to precisely pick up and position devices for testing, enhancing flexibility and accuracy. Each type caters to different production needs and efficiencies.</p></p>

<p><p>The Cold Chain Environment Monitoring Sensors market includes two primary types: wired and wireless sensors. Wired sensors provide reliable, continuous monitoring of temperature and humidity in cold chain logistics through physical connections, offering stability and low latency. However, installation can be cumbersome. In contrast, wireless sensors offer greater flexibility and ease of installation, using radio frequencies or IoT technology to transmit real-time data. They facilitate remote monitoring and can be easily integrated into existing systems, enhancing operational efficiency.</p></p>

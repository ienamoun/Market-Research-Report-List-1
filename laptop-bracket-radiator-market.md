<p><strong>The Laptop Bracket Radiator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Laptop Bracket Radiator</li><li>Passive Laptop Bracket Radiator</li><li>Multi-Purpose Laptop Bracket Radiator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laptop bracket radiator market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Active Laptop Bracket Radiator** - These models utilize fans or pumps to enhance airflow and heat dissipation, providing efficient cooling for high-performance laptops.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Passive Laptop Bracket Radiator** - Relying on natural convection and thermal conduction, passive radiators use materials that absorb and disperse heat without mechanical assistance, making them quieter and energy-efficient.</p><p>3. **Multi-Purpose Laptop Bracket Radiator** - Versatile designs that combine active and passive features, suitable for varied laptop sizes and cooling needs, often incorporating adjustable settings for optimal performance.</p></p>

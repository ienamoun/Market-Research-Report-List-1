<p><p>The metal disc coupling market is primarily categorized into single disc couplings and double disc couplings. Single disc couplings consist of a single flexible disc that allows for axial, angular, and lateral misalignments, making them suitable for compact applications. Double disc couplings feature two discs, providing enhanced flexibility and torque transmission capabilities, making them ideal for applications requiring greater misalignment compensation and load handling. Both types are essential in various industrial applications, ensuring reliable power transmission and reducing wear.</p></p>

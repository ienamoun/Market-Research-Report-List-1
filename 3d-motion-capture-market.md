<p><p>The 3D Motion Capture market is divided into two main types: Optical and Non-Optical systems. Optical systems utilize cameras and markers to track motion, providing high precision and detail, ideal for film and animation. In contrast, Non-Optical systems employ technologies like inertial sensors or magnetic fields, which are more portable and versatile, making them suitable for gaming and sports applications. Each system caters to distinct needs, balancing accuracy with flexibility in various industries.</p></p>
